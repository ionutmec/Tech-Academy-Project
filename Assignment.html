Messi

Messi won his record-extending Ballon d'Or award ahead of Manchester City forward Erling Haaland. France forward Kylian Mbappe - who became just the second man to score a World Cup final hat-trick in the 4-2 penalty shootout loss to Argentina - finished third. "It's nice to be here once more to enjoy this moment," Messi said. "To be able to win the World Cup and achieve my dream." The former Barcelona and Paris St-Germain star added: "I couldn't imagine having the career I've had and everything I've achieved, the fortune I've had to be part of the best team in history."
